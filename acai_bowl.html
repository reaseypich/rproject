Acai Bowl

breakfast
Originating from Brazil, the acai bowl is a refreshing frozen smoothie bowl that needs no introduction. Packed with antioxidants as well as essential amino and omega fatty acids, acai berries are truly a superfood. It is surprisingly easy to make your own acai bowl at home, and you can customize it with whatever toppings you prefer!
The average Acai Bowl is filled with antioxidants, vitamins, minerals, fiber, protein, and healthy fats. Acai berries also have a pretty unique nutritional profile for a fruit â€” they are low in sugar and higher in healthy fats (which is why an acai bowl fills you up for much longer than a smoothie bowl).

ACAI BOWL BASE

  • Banana: 1 frozen banana.
  • Buleberries: 1/2 cup of frozen blueberries.
  • Strawberries: 1/2 cup of frozen strawberries.
  • Acai puree: 100g frozen (1 packet).
  • Liquid: 1/4 - 1/2 cup coconut water / plant milk of choice.

TOPPINGS

  • Banana: 1/2 banana (sliced).
  • Buleberries: 1/4 cup of blueberries.
  • Strawberries: 3 strawberries (diced).
  • Chai seeds: 1 tbsp.
  • Coconut: 1 tbsp desiccated coconut.
  • Granola: Handful of granola.

BLEND

Add all ingredients for acai bowl base to a high speed blender and blend until combined.

ASSEMBLE

Pour in a bowl and top with toppings of choice. Enjoy!
